<p>A SimulationComposite is an aggregation of Simulaitons.<br /></p><p>With the aggreation connector between Simulation and SimulationComposite(SC) the SC can be made up of both SimulationRuns and SCs.<br /></p><p><br /></p><p>The SimulationComposite is the new name for the concept of SimulationCollection: A simulation can be made up of "child" simulations aggregated together to form a "simulation composite".  The "parent" simulation can be made up of whole or partial child simulations and the SimulationComposite attributes need to be able to capture this.<br /></p>
