A numerical experiment may be conducted as part of a MIP. Examples: AR4 individual experiments, AR5 individual experiments, RAPID THC experiments etc. <br /></p><p><br /></p><p>The Experiment class from v1.5 grouped together various activities which are now catered for by the observations and procedures classes and their subclasses, as well as this, NumericalExperiment.  The different aspects of the experiment need to be separated out to fit O&M: the observation that is part of the experiment, the process that is used, and the experiment itself, being the reason for conducting the observation etc.<br /></p>
